## Paragraphs

Most content on a simple web page will appear in paragraphs or sections. HTML paragraphs are defined with the `<p>` tag.

{% highlight html %}
  <p>Paragraph 1</p>
  <p>Paragraph 2</p>
  <p>Paragraph 3</p>
{% endhighlight %}
